Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 27, 2025

Welcome to WordCrypt! This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our word puzzle game ("Service"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a safe gaming experience.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

When you play WordCrypt, we collect minimal information necessary to provide the game experience:

  • Game Data: Your game guesses, progress, and statistics are stored locally on your device (mobile app) or in session cookies (web app).
  • Session Information: We use session cookies to maintain your game state during your visit to our website.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you use our Service, we may automatically collect:

  • Device Information: Basic device information may be collected to ensure compatibility and improve performance.
  • Usage Data: We may collect information about how you interact with the game to improve our services.
  • IP Address: Your IP address may be logged for security and analytics purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the game service
  • To track your game progress and statistics
  • To ensure the daily word puzzle works correctly
  • To improve and optimize the game experience
  • To detect and prevent technical issues

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Web Application

When you play WordCrypt on our website (wordcrypt.in), your game state is stored in session cookies that expire when you close your browser. No personal information is stored on our servers.

3.2 Mobile Application

When you use the WordCrypt mobile app, your game statistics and preferences are stored locally on your device using secure local storage. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to sync it (if such feature is added in the future).

3.3 Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information only in the following circumstances:

  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or in response to valid legal requests.
  • Service Providers: We may use third-party services to host our website, but they do not have access to your game data.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred.

5. Third-Party Services

Our Service may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. We encourage you to read their privacy policies.

6. Children's Privacy

WordCrypt is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete such information.

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your information:

  • Access: You can view your game statistics at any time within the app.
  • Deletion: You can clear your game data by clearing your browser cookies (web) or app data (mobile).
  • Opt-Out: You can stop using the Service at any time.

8. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use session cookies to maintain your game state. These cookies are essential for the game to function properly and are deleted when you close your browser. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party advertising cookies.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
